# Brand / Style Guide

Use this skill to keep content consistent with a brand's voice and rules.

## Voice and tone
- Define the brand personality (e.g., friendly, expert, concise).
- Adjust tone by context: reassuring in support, energetic in marketing.
- Write for the audience's level of expertise.

## Mechanics
- Capitalization and title case rules.
- Numbers, dates, and units formatting.
- Preferred and banned terms.
- Oxford comma: choose one convention and keep it.

## Workflow
1. Load the brand's voice rules and terminology list.
2. Draft content, then check against the guide.
3. Flag deviations and suggest compliant rewrites.
