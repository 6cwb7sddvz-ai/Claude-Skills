# Meeting Notes to Action Items

Use this skill to process meeting transcripts or raw notes.

## Output format
- Summary: 2-3 sentences on what the meeting covered.
- Decisions: bullet list of what was decided.
- Action items: task, owner, and due date for each.
- Open questions: unresolved items needing follow-up.

## Guidelines
- Attribute action items to a named owner.
- Capture due dates explicitly; flag if missing.
- Keep the summary neutral and factual.

## Workflow
1. Read the full transcript or notes.
2. Extract decisions and commitments.
3. Assign owners and dates to each action item.
4. Produce the structured summary above.
